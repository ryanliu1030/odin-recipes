Description:

Tasty, quick, chicken thighs, with juicy meat and a crispy skin; you will love this chicken that is ready for the table in less than 30 minutes!

Ingredients:

    2 teaspoons extra-virgin olive oil 1 teaspoon smoked parika 3/4 teaspoon garlic powder

Steps:

  1. 400 F Preheat Fryer
  2. Combine all the seasoning into bowl and mix them
  3. Pat the chicken thigh
